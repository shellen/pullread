Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 19, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Pull Read, a product of A Little Drive LLC ("A Little Drive", "we", "us", "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use Pull Read (the "Software"). For more information about using the Software, please refer to our Terms of Use.

2. Local-first by design

Pull Read is designed to keep your data on your device. Articles, highlights, notes, tags, and reading history are stored locally on your Mac in the output folder you configure and in ~/.config/pullread/. We do not operate servers that store your data.

3. What stays local

  • All saved articles (Markdown files in your output folder)
  • Highlights, notes, and annotations
  • Tags and favorites
  • Reading history and preferences
  • API keys (stored locally in your config directory)
  • TTS audio cache (stored locally in .tts-cache/)
  • Browser TTS — fully on-device

4. When data leaves your device

Data is only sent to third parties when you explicitly use certain optional features:

  • AI Summaries & Auto-tagging: When you click Summarize or run auto-tagging, article text is sent to your selected LLM provider (Anthropic, OpenAI, Google Gemini, or OpenRouter) using your own API key. Apple Intelligence processes on-device.
  • Weekly Reviews: When scheduled or triggered manually, recent article content is sent to your configured LLM provider.
  • Cloud TTS (OpenAI / ElevenLabs): When you use a paid TTS provider, article text is sent to that provider's API using your dedicated TTS API key. A consent prompt is shown before the first use, and cost estimates are displayed.
  • RSS Feed Fetching: Pull Read fetches RSS/Atom feeds and article URLs you configure. These are standard HTTP requests to the content sources.
  • Auto-updates: The macOS app checks for updates via a periodic HTTPS request to GitHub Releases. No personal data is included in this request.

Each third-party provider has its own privacy policy governing how they handle data sent through their APIs. We strongly advise you to review those policies.

5. API keys

API keys are stored locally in ~/.config/pullread/settings.json. They are never sent to us, never logged, and never shared between features (your Summaries key is separate from your TTS key). Keys are transmitted only to the provider API you selected, over HTTPS.

6. The viewer server

Pull Read's built-in article reader runs a local HTTP server bound to 127.0.0.1 (localhost only). It is not accessible from other devices on your network.

7. Analytics and tracking

Pull Read does not include any analytics, telemetry, crash reporting, or tracking. We do not collect any usage data.

8. Email collection (pullread.com)

When you download Pull Read or subscribe to product updates on our website, we collect your email address and, for downloads, which platform (Apple Silicon or Intel) you selected.

  • Purpose & legal basis: Download delivery (legitimate interest — fulfilling your request) and product update emails (consent — opt-in checkbox, unchecked by default).
  • Data stored: Email address, source (download or newsletter), platform, and timestamp.
  • Processor & location: Stored in Cloudflare D1 (encrypted at rest) on Cloudflare's global network. Data may be processed in the United States and other countries where Cloudflare operates. Cloudflare acts as a data processor under their privacy policy and Data Processing Addendum.
  • Retention: We retain your email until you request deletion or unsubscribe. Unsubscribed records are soft-deleted (marked inactive) and permanently purged within 90 days.
  • No sharing: We do not sell or share your email with third parties.

You can unsubscribe from product updates at any time via the link included in every email. To request access, correction, portability, or deletion of your data, contact support@alittledrive.com.

9. Data security

Because Pull Read stores data locally on your device, data security is primarily in your hands. We recommend keeping your operating system and the Software up to date. No method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of locally stored data.

10. Your choices and rights

Because Pull Read is local-first, you have direct control over your data at all times. You can delete any article, note, highlight, or configuration file directly from your filesystem. Uninstalling the Software and removing ~/.config/pullread/ removes all traces of Pull Read from your device.

10a. California privacy rights

California residents have certain rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA), including the right to know what personal information is collected, the right to delete it, and the right to opt out of its sale. The Software itself is local-first and does not transmit personal data to our servers. The only personal information we collect is email addresses submitted through our website (see Section 8). We do not sell personal information. To exercise your rights, contact support@alittledrive.com.

11. Children's privacy

Pull Read is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe we may have collected information from your child, please contact us immediately at support@alittledrive.com.

12. International users and GDPR

Pull Read is operated from the United States. If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or Switzerland, you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including the right to access, rectify, port, or erase your personal data, and the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. Our lawful bases for processing are consent (newsletter) and legitimate interest (download delivery). To exercise any of these rights, contact support@alittledrive.com. By submitting your email through our website, you acknowledge that your data will be transferred to and processed in the United States.

13. Changes to this policy

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. If we make changes, we will notify you by updating the "Effective date" at the top of this page. Your continued use of the Software after any modifications indicates your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
