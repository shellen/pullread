Roadmap

Where Pull Read is headed.

Pull Read is built in the open. Here's what we're working on, what's coming next, and where we want to go. Priorities can shift based on feedback — if something here matters to you, let us know.

In progress

Now

Knowledge graph explorer

A visual way to browse connections between your articles by topic, author, and source. See how your reading connects.

intelligence

Onboarding tour

A guided setup flow for new users — connect your first feed, learn the keyboard shortcuts, and configure AI in under two minutes.

experience

Sidebar redesign

A cleaner, more flexible sidebar with collapsible sections, drag-to-reorder feeds, and faster navigation.

experience
Up next

Next

iOS companion app

Read your library on your phone. Syncs via iCloud Drive or any shared folder. Offline reading with highlights and notes.

platform

Obsidian sync

Bidirectional sync between Pull Read annotations and Obsidian notes. Your highlights and tags flow both ways.

integrations

More ebook formats

Mobi, PDF, and other document formats join EPUB in your reading library. Same highlights, summaries, and search.

content

CJK and internationalization

Full support for Chinese, Japanese, and Korean text rendering, plus localized UI for major languages.

global

Later

Windows and Linux

Pull Read is built on Tauri, which supports all desktop platforms. Windows and Linux ports are on the horizon.

platform

Web login and cloud sync

Optional account-based sync for people who want their library everywhere without managing shared folders.

platform

Audio player upgrades

Playlist queues, variable speed per-feed, sleep timers, and CarPlay integration for listening on the go.
