What's New

Release notes for Pull Read, from first light to the latest build.

0.4.0 "Connecting the dots"
February 2026
  • Knowledge graph — Related Reading suggestions on every article
  • Your Topics — see what you read about most, powered by auto-tagging
  • Topic Clusters in the Tags tab — browse related tags together
  • For You dashboard with Daily Rundown, continue reading, and reviews
  • Redesigned audio player with Apple Music-style miniplayer
  • Three-state API key fields — see what's saved without exposing secrets
  • Auto-save on all settings — no more Save buttons
  • Browser TTS seeking and auto-advance to next article
  • Fixed Substack empty article bodies
  • XKCD comic strip rendering with hover-text captions
0.3.5 "Your annotations, your files"
February 23, 2026
  • Annotations stored as portable .annot.json sidecars alongside each article
  • Existing highlights, notes, tags, and favorites auto-migrated on first launch
  • Sidebar responsiveness fix when switching from Notebooks back to articles
  • Dark mode highlight text contrast — no more invisible highlights
  • Notebooks tab always shows New Note button and notes list
  • Fixed navigation from Settings to articles and notes in sidebar
  • FAQ page at pullread.com/faq
0.3.4 "The one where feeds fight back"
February 23, 2026
  • Feed health monitoring — broken feeds show errors in Settings with a Retry button
  • Smart feed discovery — paste a YouTube channel or Reddit URL, we'll find the RSS
  • PDF export with section picker
  • Article-to-PDF export
  • Fixed blank page on Intel Macs when the server needs a moment
  • Fixed bracket rendering in articles (looking at you, SFist)
  • Log rotation so your temp folder doesn't become a novel
0.3.3 "Looking good on the internet"
February 23, 2026
  • Self-hosted fonts for fully offline reading
  • Bookmarklet gets its own step in the setup tour
  • Rich link previews when you share pullread.com
0.3.2 "Plumbing"
February 22, 2026
  • Fixed auto-updater signing and manifest generation
0.3.1 "The EPUB chapter"
February 22, 2026
  • EPUB book support with chapter navigation and table of contents
  • Cover images and footnote popups
  • Fixed filename collisions when articles share a title
  • Internationalization groundwork (FR, IT, DE, ES)
0.3.0 "The big rearrange"
February 21, 2026
  • Articles organized in dated subfolders (auto-migrated from flat layout)
  • Redesigned Settings page
  • Voice dictation for notebook notes
  • Custom button groups replace all dropdowns
  • Recommended feeds in Settings
  • Break reminders with bundled Gutenberg reading
  • Video embeds for tweets and articles
0.2.1 "The legal eagle"

  • Direct DMG download links
0.2.0 "Hello, world"
